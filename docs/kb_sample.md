# Sample Knowledge Base

## Returns Policy

Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unopened and in
original packaging. Refund processing takes 5–7 business days after the item is
received at our warehouse.

## Shipping

Standard shipping is 3–5 business days within the US. Express shipping is
1–2 business days. International shipping is 7–14 business days; customs fees
are the responsibility of the recipient.

## Support Hours

Phone support is available Monday through Friday, 9am–6pm Eastern Time.
Email support replies within 24 hours on business days.
